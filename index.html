About DPS Global School

At DPS Global School, Baripada we focus on clear teaching, consistent practice, and real-world skills. Students learn in a calm, supportive environment with strong routines, caring mentorship, and plenty of opportunities in sports, arts, and clubs.

Our Mission

To develop disciplined, curious learners through strong academics, skills-based learning, and values that show up every day—in class, on the field, and at home.

Our Vision

To be Baripada’s trusted school for future-ready education—where students grow in knowledge, confidence, and responsibility in a fast-changing world.

Our Values

Integrity, respect, effort, and empathy guide our community—helping children build strong habits, meaningful friendships, and a positive mindset.
